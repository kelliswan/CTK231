About Me

I am a music artist from Southern California. I write soul-shaking lyrics and have a soulful voice that pierces the heart. I write very emotional content that connects with others on many levels and can prove to be very cathartic. I play guitar and produce. I have a lot of unreleased music. This is the season of my musical rebirth.
